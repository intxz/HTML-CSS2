Vegetta777

Samuel De Luque, one of the most famous youtubers at the start of Youtube Gaming.

Projects

  • Minecraft Series: Throughout his career, Vegetta777 has created different Minecraft series, each with a different focus. Among the most popular series we can highlight Planet Vegetta, the various seasons of Karmaland with other creators, Minecraft Apocalypse, Minecraft with Noobs and Pirates (also together with other content creators, such as Alexby, HeRny, Arsilex, etc.).
  • KarmaRun: Along with Willyrex and sTaXx, Vegetta launched a video game in collaboration with the video game developer U-Play Online. This is an endless runner type video game in which the player must run through a long path while avoiding obstacles and earning coins. It is available on both Google Play and the App Store.
  • Wigetta Books: Together with Willyrex, Vegetta777 has published a series of books for children and young people that narrate the adventures of its two characters, Vegetta and Willy, who are accompanied by their pets Vakypandy and Trotuman. In their adventures they meet various characters and find themselves involved in different situations that require a solution. The first book in this saga was published in 2015, and as of 2022, 18 books have been published.
  • Mad Lions: In collaboration with Willyrex, Vegetta became one of the majority partners of MAD Lions, a Spanish esports team.
  • Cartoons: Willyrex and Vegetta published the chapters of Wigetta, an animated series with 2D design within the YouTube platform on a channel with the same name. Within the series the adventures of the characters Willyrex and Vegetta are shown, who find themselves in situations in which they must help certain characters fulfill their wishes, while they themselves fulfill theirs. The first chapter obtained many visits from the community of both YouTubers, as well as the subsequent chapters. Until 2022, 10 chapters of the series have been uploaded.
